About Ascensions 2020

ASCENSIONS 2020 is an extravagant, online inter- collegiate Fest that is hosted across 14 days by YUVA REC, the student wing of the Young Indians Movement in collaboration with the Confederation of Indian Industries, at Rajalakshmi Engineering College, Chennai. Ascensions aims to be a forerunner of developing visions and principles. It plots a course for students to instill in themselves, a sense of social involvement and carves the way to an atmosphere where notions appear, revolutionize and lead to new opportunities. We serve to be a union of different streams of thoughts, expressions and cultures that find their ways to conquer infinite milestones, ultimately focused on creating a better tomorrow. Along with many intellectually stimulating sessions, Ascensions has a multifaceted roster of events that enables young minds to unleash their inner passions, free from any inhibitions. In 2020, we the organisers strive to include all institutions near and far, in an attempt to create an extensive impact, by raising awareness for the noble cause of Organ Donation, in the pursuit to save as many lives as possible all while creating unforgettable memories and a thrilling escape during these trying times. We boast of an one of its kind initiative to spread awareness and save lives. Question is, will you join us?
